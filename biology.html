BRAIN
Understanding the neural circuits underlying behavior is at the core of my research. I focus on how sensory information is processed and transformed into meaningful behavioral outputs, with particular emphasis on mosquito olfactory systems and their response to human cues.
BEHAVIOR
My research investigates complex behavioral patterns in insects, particularly focusing on mosquito swarming dynamics and host-seeking behaviors. Using advanced tracking systems and behavioral analysis, we study how environmental cues influence these behaviors at both individual and group levels.
TOOLS
We develop and implement cutting-edge tools for neuroscience research, including advanced imaging techniques, machine learning algorithms for behavioral analysis, and custom-built hardware solutions for tracking and stimulus delivery. Our toolkit combines traditional neuroscience methods with modern computational approaches.
ENVIRONMENT
Environmental factors play a crucial role in shaping animal behavior. Our research examines how various environmental conditions influence neural processing and behavioral responses, with particular attention to how climate change might affect insect behavior and human-insect interactions.
